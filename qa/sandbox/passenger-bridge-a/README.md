# passenger-bridge fixture (committed real photos — NOT generated)

Three real downscaled burst frames that reproduce the **#538 "passenger"**: a
group whose union-find closure leaves **two Ref-tier rows**, so one renders as a
"passenger" (#536 Direction A). Used by `qa/scenarios/s65_passenger_bridge.py`.

Unlike every other `qa/sandbox/` fixture (synthetic, generated by
`scripts/make_qa_sandbox.py`), these are **committed static files** — they can't
be generated. A passenger needs a *bridge*: `scene_bridge` is a genuine near-dup
of **both** `scene_left` and `scene_right` on pHash **and** dHash, while
`scene_left ≁ scene_right`. A synthetic blend fails the dHash gate (#524) — its
dHash favours the heavier endpoint — and a pan didn't hit the narrow both-hash
window (see #544). Real consecutive burst frames (a moving subject) are the
reliable source.

`d(right,bridge)=8`; dHash `d(left,bridge)=3`, `d(right,bridge)=5`.

GPS / camera / timestamp metadata is committed. ~22 KB each.
